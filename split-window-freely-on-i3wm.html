Split Window Freely on i3wm

With the following configurations in ~/.i3/config, you can create a new window (split) at the exact place you want, without moving them after created:

bindsym $mod+i split h
bindsym $mod+v split v
bindsym $mod+a focus parent

See section 3. Tree in i3 User’s Guide for examples.
